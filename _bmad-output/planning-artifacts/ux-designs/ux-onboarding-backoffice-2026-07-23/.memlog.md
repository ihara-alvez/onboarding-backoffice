- (decision) MAJOR PIVOT on card disclosure pattern: reverting the 'summary + View X drill-in link' pattern adopted earlier. New pattern: every reviewable card (all except Progress) shows its FULL content expanded by default (no truncation, no separate detail view), and gets a GitHub-PR-style 'Viewed' checkbox in its header. Checking it collapses the card down to a compact single-line bar (title only), mirroring GitHub's 'Files changed' review-tracking UX. All cards start expanded/unchecked; the user manually collapses ones they've reviewed. This replaces drill-in links as the density mechanism -- density now comes from progressive manual collapse during a review session, not permanently hidden detail behind a click-through. Applies to: First tasks, Project context, Repositories, Permissions, Checklists, Suggested documentation, Approvals and risks. Progress is exempt (live status widget, not reviewable plan content).
